GDPR Policumbent Simulator

Personal data we collect

Policumbent collects data from you through the WHPSC race simulator. You provide some of this data directly and we get some of it by collecting data about your interaction with the WHPSC race simulator.

We collect the following data:
  • Your name : provided by you at the end of the virtual race. You can post any name you like.
  • Your email address : provided by you at the end of the virtual race.

Other data we collect

With the results of your run we also store the settings you used to run the virtual WHPSC race. This includes : your height, your weight, your average power and the other technical settings for the simulator. You can also choose to leave this in default.

How we use personal data

We use the data to record and display all the runs that people make on the virtual WHPSC. We will send an email informing you of the end of the event and the final virtual gathering. We will not share, sell, store or otherwise abuse or use the data you provide.

How to access and control your personal data

Send us an email at software@policumbent.it with your request to be removed from our records.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to store data that can be recalled by a web server in the domain that placed the cookie. We use cookies and similar technologies for storing and honoring your preferences and settings.

We use Google Analytics (Google Inc.) to analyse visitor statistics for our site.

You have a variety of tools to control the data collected by cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies. For example, you can use controls in your internet browser to limit how the websites you visit are able to use cookies and to withdraw your consent by clearing or blocking cookies.

Questions?

Contact us via software@policumbent.it and we are happy to help.
