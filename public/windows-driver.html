Windows Driver - Policumbent Simulator

  1. Download Zadig from here
  2. Open Zadig
  3. Click on Options and then check List All Devices
  4. Select ANT USBStick2
  5. Select libusb-win32 (v1.2.6.0)
  6. Click on Replace Driver or on Downgrade driver
  7. Wait, the process can last up to 5 minutes
  8. Close Zadig and open WHPSC Simulator App
